About
We are scàm clothing.
Social Clothing and Menswear

Literally, it means a fraudulent or deceptive act or operation. But scàm is so much more than that. By embodying scam, we are using clothing to bring out what is deceptively hidden away, personality and expression. Scàm believes in quality, whether that be in the material of our clothing, attention to our design or even the technology we leverage to bring our products to you. Everything we do is simply the best.

Scàm is an American men's clothing brand founded by 4 college students, based out San Francisco. Scàm’s free-thinking vision has long been at the heart of the movement merging the rebellious vitality of skate, surf, and music cultures with an innovative appreciation of classic menswear. Scàm was created to realize this vision fully, in uncompromising pursuit of quality, integrity, and originality.

The interests and influences that inspire our clothing fall under a large, inclusive, cross-generational umbrella. Our ideas come as much from the possibilities we see in fabrics and designs as our commitment to living active, engaged lifestyles. We believe in doing things our own way, with conviction, according to principles we determine ourselves.
